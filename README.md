 🚗 ParkzIN — Redefining Urban Mobility
ParkzIN is a futuristic urban mobility platform designed to simplify how people move, park, rent, and power their vehicles in the modern city ecosystem.  
It’s more than just a parking app — it’s a complete digital mobility ecosystem built for smart cities.
🌐 Live Demo: [parkzin.vercel.app](https://parkzin.vercel.app)

 🚀 Vision
To build a connected, sustainable, and intelligent mobility network that transforms how urban residents navigate, park, and charge vehicles — all through a single, seamless platform.

 💡 Problem Statement
Urban areas face chronic issues of:
•	Endless searching for parking spots 🅿️  
•	Limited EV charging infrastructure ⚡  
•	Unorganized valet & rental services 🚙  
•	Rising congestion and carbon footprint 🌍  
Traditional systems operate in silos, leading to wasted time, energy, and resources.

 💼 The ParkzIN Solution 
ParkzIN unifies five major services into a single platform:

Feature	Description
Smart Parking:	Find, reserve, and navigate to available parking spots in realtime.

EV Charging: 	Discover nearby charging stations and monitor live charging availability.

Carpooling:	Connect with nearby commuters and reduce travel costs and emissions.

Valet Assistance: 	Book a valet who can pick up, park, or retrieve your vehicle securely. 

Vehicle & Cycle Rentals:	Rent vehicles or ecofriendly cycles to move around the city conveniently.
